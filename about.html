About Seval Gıda

About Us

Your Trusted Global Food Partner Since 1984

Seval Gıda is a leading Turkish food manufacturer specialized in international exports and B2B partnerships. With nearly four decades of experience, we provide high-quality, Halal-certified products to importers, wholesalers, and HORECA (Hotel, Restaurant, Catering) professionals around the world. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure hygienic, hands-free production processes that fully comply with international food safety standards. With ISO 9001, ISO 22000, ISO 45001, ISO 14001 and Halal certifications, Seval Gıda guarantees consistent quality and safety in every product we deliver.

We are committed to building long-term partnerships with distributors and importers by offering reliable logistics, flexible packaging solutions, and a customer-centric approach that makes us a preferred business partner worldwide.

As a company focused on global exports, Seval Gıda continuously invests in innovation and technology to expand its international reach. Our wide product portfolio is designed to meet the dynamic demands of different markets, while ensuring competitive pricing and superior quality for our partners.

Sustainability, transparency, and trust are the core principles of our business. By prioritizing environmental responsibility and ethical production, we not only meet the expectations of today’s global markets but also contribute to a healthier and safer future for the next generations.
